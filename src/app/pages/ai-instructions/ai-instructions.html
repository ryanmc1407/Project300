How to Use AI Task Generation

Be specific and include context about your project. For example:

  • Good: "Create 5 tasks for building a user authentication system with registration, login, email verification, and password reset"
  • Good: "Break down the frontend redesign project into tasks. Assign design tasks to Sarah and dev tasks to John. Deadline: next Friday"
  • Avoid: Vague prompts like "Create some tasks"

Mention team member names in your prompt ‚Äî the AI will try to automatically assign tasks to them based on their names. You can always edit assignments before confirming.

Include time references like "by Friday", "within 2 weeks", or specific dates like "by March 15th". The AI will parse these and set appropriate due dates on the generated tasks.

After generation, review the draft tasks carefully. You can remove tasks you don't need by checking the checkboxes. Click "Create All Tasks" to save them to your project, or regenerate with a different prompt if you're not satisfied.
